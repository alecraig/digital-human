Ducks are a popular animal in this world, and is seen in various places and/or in media. From seeing it in nature, as rubber duck toys, to shooting galleries, ducks are everywhere. In addition to these, ducks can be seen:

  • nature
  • rubber ducks
  • shooting gallery ducks
  • duck characters "Donald Duck, Daffy Duck"
  • video game ducks "Ducklett, Duck Hunt"

And no, a swan is NOT a duck even though a Ducklett can evolve to Swanna in the game "Pokemon".

 

Need to know the differences between a duck and a swan? No problem! Take a look at this infographic:

Ducks Swans
Ducks are small creatures. Swans are larger in size.
Ducks are not as aggressive to humans. Swans can be territorial and can attack humans.
Ducks live from four to eight years. Swans live from eight to twelve years.
